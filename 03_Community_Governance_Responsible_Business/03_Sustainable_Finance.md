## What is Sustainable Finance?

Sustainable finance refers to financial products and services that integrate environmental, social, and governance (ESG) considerations into investment and lending decisions.


### Key Sustainable Finance Instruments

| Instrument | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| **Green bonds** | Raise capital specifically for environmental projects |
| **Green loans** | Borrowing tied to environmental use of funds |
| **Sustainability-linked loans** | Interest rates tied to ESG performance |
| **ESG-focused investment** | Investment based on sustainability criteria |

### Why Sustainable Finance Matters

- **Funds transition**: Provides capital for clean energy investment
- **Signals commitment**: Demonstrates long-term sustainability focus
- **Attracts investors**: Meets growing demand for ESG investments
- **Reduces costs**: Can lower cost of capital for green projects

## Contact's Sustainable Finance Milestones

Contact has achieved several industry firsts in sustainable finance:

### 2017: New Zealand's First Green Borrowing Programme

> **First in New Zealand:** Contact established the first Green Borrowing Programme in New Zealand.

This groundbreaking programme allows us to raise debt financing specifically for environmentally beneficial projects.

**How It Works:**
- Funds are earmarked for green projects
- Projects must meet defined environmental criteria
- Progress is reported transparently
- Third-party verification ensures compliance

**Eligible Projects Include:**
- Renewable energy generation (geothermal, solar, wind)
- Energy efficiency improvements
- Biodiversity and ecosystem protection
- Water management infrastructure

### 2019: Sustainability-Linked Loan

Contact established a sustainability-linked loan, with terms tied to our ESG performance.

**How It Works:**
- Interest rate adjusts based on ESG metrics
- Strong ESG performance = lower borrowing costs
- Creates financial incentive for sustainability
- Demonstrates that sustainability creates value

**Benefits:**
- Aligns financial and sustainability objectives
- Provides concrete rewards for ESG improvement
- Signals to markets our commitment to sustainability
- Reduces cost of capital for good performance

### 2020: First Asia-Pacific Company on Nasdaq Sustainable Bond Network

> **First in Asia-Pacific:** Contact became the first company in the entire Asia-Pacific region to list bonds on Nasdaq's Sustainable Bond Network.

This significant achievement brought international recognition to our green finance credentials.

**What This Means:**
- Global visibility for our green bonds
- Access to international sustainable investors
- Recognition of our green finance framework
- Leadership position in sustainable capital markets
